UNIVERSAL ORLANDO

Your Gateway to The Movies

ABOUT

Universal Orlando Resort is an immersive vacation destination where incredible adventures await. With two theme parks, Universal Studios Florida and Islands of Adventure, you can journey into the worlds of your favorite movies and stories. From the magic of The Wizarding World of Harry Potter to epic thrills like The Incredible Hulk Coaster, there's something for everyone. Plus, with the addition of the Volcano Bay water park and the vibrant entertainment complex at Universal CityWalk, your Orlando adventure extends beyond the theme parks, ensuring a vacation filled with excitement, entertainment, and unforgettable moments. Universal Orlando Resort invites you to explore, indulge, and celebrate the thrill of discovery.

Beyond the attractions, Universal Orlando Resort offers a diverse array of dining options, from themed restaurants to quick-service eateries, allowing you to refuel and savor a wide range of cuisines. At Universal CityWalk, you can unwind, enjoy a meal, catch a movie, or dance the night away, creating the perfect ending to your day of adventures. Whether you're a family seeking a magical journey, a thrill-seeker in search of heart-pounding excitement, or a foodie looking for culinary delights, Universal Orlando Resort promises an extraordinary vacation experience that caters to your every desire.

WIZARDING WORLD OF MAGIC

Diagon Alley in Universal Studios Florida and Hogsmeade in Islands of Adventure. You can take the Hogwarts Express between them and enjoy iconic attractions.

PIC THRILLS AND ADVENTURE

The Incredible Hulk, the Jurassic Park River Adventure, and the adrenaline-pumping Hollywood Rip Ride Rockit.

VOLCANO BAY WATER PARK

Tropical-themed water park filled with exhilarating water slides, a relaxing lazy river, and the centerpiece Krakatau Volcano.

CITYWALK

The entertainment and dining complex, offers a wide range of restaurants, bars, shops, and entertainment venues.
